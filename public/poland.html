Illusion House

On our First day we went to an illusion house. In an illusion house or museum, visitors can explore various rooms or exhibits that use optical illusions, visual tricks, and interactive displays to create the perception of distorted space, gravity-defying scenes, and other mind-bending effects. These attractions often provide a fun and immersive experience for visitors and can be a popular form of entertainment and education.
